Why chose us?

On your luxury wild safari explore true unchartered wilderness, by foot or by 4×4 . Stay in one of the world’s most luxurious and intimate safari lodges and camps. See the wildebeest migration as it takes its annual route through the eighth wonder of the world, the Masai Mara. Watch lions, leopards and cheetah hunt down unsuspecting prey. Learn the Masai and Samburu traditions, and dance around the campfire. Retreat to the azure inviting waters of the Indian Ocean.

On this safari, witness the Great Migration, as hundreds of thousands of grazing animals and predators cross the plains of Kenya and Tanzania. Venture to Southern Africa for water safaris by dugout canoe through Botswana's Okavango Delta, and for a visit to mighty Victoria Falls in Zimbabwe.

Tranquil and luxurious, the camps enjoys uninterrupted views of the Uaso Nyiro River from the private verandas of every spacious and stylish guest tent.

Smartly, strategically placed in 50 acres of lush, untrammeled Tanzanian bush a short drive from Ngorongoro’s rim, the lodge features deliciously private luxury log cabin suites kitted out with stunning modern and a private viewing terrace for taking in the transcendent wooden floors and furnishings, an open fireplace, early-morning radiance of Tanzania’s highlands.

While you’re in the area, you might be interested in these fine extensions like hot air balloon safari, tracking majestic mountain gorillas etc. Our travel specialists can work them into your itinerary.
